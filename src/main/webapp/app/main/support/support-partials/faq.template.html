I have no sound coming from my speakers what should I do?

Make sure that your speaker cord is in the proper output jack. If you have a internal sound card installed and are not using the built in audio make sure that your speakers are connected to the audio card and not the onboard connection near the USB ports.

Check your volume with the volume control on the lower right hand on the windows toolbar. The icon is a speaker, make sure the speakers are not muted and that the speakers are powered on.

I have no video signal what should I do?

First, check all cable connections from your rig to your monitors are not loose and are properly connected.

Second, confirm that you are connecting your monitors to the video card and not the onboard video output on your motherboard.

Third, monitors have an input selection button. Make sure to cycle through all inputs to verify that you are in fact on the right input device ie. (HDMI,DVI,VGA)

Where do I download my video drivers NVIDA/AMD?

You may download NVIDIA video drivers from http://www.nvidia.com/Download/index.aspx?lang=en-us make sure to select the correct video card series.

You may download AMD video drivers from http://support.amd.com/en-us make sure to select the correct video card series.

I am getting CPU Fan error on boot up, what should I do?

This error is most commonly due to the usage of Dark Fusion Liquid Cooling in our rigs. To turn the error off enter the bios, switch it to advanced mode, from there find the Monitor tab. When you have arrived at the Monitor tab set the CPU Fan Speed to Ignore. If you need any further assistance please contact Shadow Rigs Support.
