We are Shadow Rigs

We are Shadow Rigs but above all we are PC gamers. Our main goal is to provide the PC Gaming community with the tools and the equipment to have the best gaming experience possible, we design our systems in such a way that they can be upgradable for the user to stay on the cutting edge of PC gaming. Our community is extremely important to us and we aim to give back as much as we can. At Shadow Rigs we are all gamers with an intense passion for the superior experience you can achieve with a PC. We are driven by an inclusive PC gaming community with a dedicated, unwavering focus on expansion to offer this amazing community what it deserves.
